About

I'm an Aerospace Engineering student at UC San Diego concentrating in flight dynamics and controls, with a strong focus on fluid mechanics, computational fluid dynamics, and finite element methods. I work across the whole pipeline, from theory and simulation to hands-on design and manufacturing, turning models into parts that actually get built and tested. When I'm not building, I play tennis, run, and read, lately about vehicle dynamics and low Reynolds number hydrodynamics.
